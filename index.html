Every family has its own traditional recipes, passed down from generation to generation. Secret Family Recipes takes your cooking traditions out of the notecard box in the pantry and into the palm of your hand. Share your recipes with friends and family anywhere with your own personal tips and tricks. Create and edit your own versions of classic meals to leave a personalized mark on the heritage of your recipe book. Secret Family Recipes makes your family recipes more convenient than ever.
